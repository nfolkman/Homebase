About Me

Hey there! My name's Nathan.

Having been around the world, I’ve had the opportunity to be involved in a vibrant selection of both community and corporate projects. In every case, the common thread that pulls us through is people. As a software engineer, I’m a problem-solver, but that’s not without context. Whether it's providing a more adaptable interface for a dedicated team of researchers, or adapting a database to be more efficient for a given user population: I build for people.

Below, I have a selection of projects, some of which are mine and function as a portfolio of my work. However, in my experience, the most treacherous obstacle to meaningful experiences is finding them. To that end, I also provide links to and information about projects that, while not involving me, have inspired me. And my hope is that they can inspire you as well.
